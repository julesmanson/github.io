about me

I really don't have floppy ears. That's my by best friend Miles. I am an engineer skilled in mechanical design with AutoCAD, Fusion 360, Inventor, SolidWorks, and CATIA V5. I am also skilled in geometric dimensioning and tolerancing by ASME Y14.5. In my free time I love coding frontend web applications that help find solutions to engineering problems.

This entire site was hand coded using only Vanilla Javascript and raw CSS. This means that no templates, frameworks, libraries, preprocessors, or transpilers were used unless otherwise listed under credits below. However all best practices including functional programming and applicable modern programming design patterns (see Design Patterns by Gang of Four) were observed. To learn more about me please view my resume in contact page.
